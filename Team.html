Meet the Team

Owner/CFO

Batman

After a long night of defending Gotham City, Batman realized the need for a tasty donut shop with java pick-me-up in a relaxing enviornment. Realizing that he needed a day job Batman created and opened Glazed Over Donuts and Coffee. A graduate of Gotham University with a degree in Economics Glazed Over Donuts now has 3 locations around the Tri-City Area.

Head Chef/ Donut Maker

Aquaman

Realizing his love for baking at a young age, Aquaman perfected his techique around the world before settling in Gotham City to join Batman in making Glazed Over the success it is. Winner of multiple baking competitions and prestigous awards, Aquaman's skill and love of pastery really shows in his specialty donuts
